Every individual has different tastes and preferences, but here are a few ways I have been able to save during my travels to adhere to my budget and make traveling sustainable for me: 1. Try flying out of different airports. The cost of driving a little bit to another State close by or a different airport not too far away can help save significant costs. From Ann Arbor, the Detroit airport DTW is good, but Toronto (YYZ), Chicago (ORD), and other airports in Ohio are worth looking to fly from. 2. Finding the right amount of people to go with (whether friends or family), can make the trip more fun and can decrease the cost per person for accommodation. 3. Travel at off-peak times and do research to pick the right time to book. 2. Monitor Google Flights as much as possible to get the best deals. Utilize the map function to find new places you might not have considered at a price you are happy with.
